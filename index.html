Microdle

How to Play

Identify the hidden food microbe in 8 tries.

Microbes can be pathogens, spoilage organisms, or fermentation organisms. Names vary in length.

V

V is in the microbe and in the correct spot.

I

I is in the microbe but in the wrong spot.

B

B is not in the microbe in any spot.

Stuck? A Run Lab Assay hint button appears after your 2nd incorrect guess to reveal the microbe's organism type.
